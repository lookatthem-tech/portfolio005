iTips for Searching

For the most successful searches:

l Put quotation marks around searches with multiple words. For example, searching for "time off" finds only pages that include that exact combination of words. It does not find overtime, timesheet, etc.

l When you search for a single word, be as specific as possible. For example, timesheet would be better than time. Searching for time finds pages that contain time, overtime, timely, or timesheet.

l Use the Filter button in the Search box to filter the topics in which you search.

Verifying Broadcast Zone

  1. Sign in to the Prysm Application Suite.
  2. In the meeting controller, press the share screen icon, the Broadcast Zone should appear.
  3. If it does appear the Broadcast Zone is properly configured.
  4. This zone can be moved anywhere on the display and scaled larger or smaller. Its maximized size is the height of the display and it cannot be moved outside of the display borders. Try moving it around on the display.
  5. The bottom toolbar on this zone has 6 buttons.
    1. Close - This button closes the Broadcast Zone
    2. Share - This button takes a screenshot of everything in the Broadcast Zone and sends it to the specified e-mail addresses
    3. Snapshot - This button takes a screenshot of everything in the Broadcast Zone and saves it to your project files
    4. Add Participant - If there are no participants in the meeting, this button appears in place of the begin broadcasting button
    5. Broadcast (Play) - When pressed, this button will broadcast (play) everything inside of the Broadcasts Zone to participants in your Skype for Business call. When broadcasting, the bottom left of the Broadcast Zone tool bar will display a green ON AIR icon and the user will not be able to move or re-size the Broadcast Zone. Stopping broadcasting by pressing the button a second time will release the Broadcast Zone and allow it to be moved or re-sized.
    6. Stop - The Stop button displays when the Broadcast Zone is broadcasting indicating the next action that can be taken by the user. When the Stop button is pressed, the broadcasting stops and the icon changes to the Broadcast (e.) button. Once the Stop button is pressed, the area to be broadcast can be moved and re-sized. Press the Broadcast button again to begin broadcasting again.

Broadcast Zone Limitations

The Broadcast Zone can output a maximum resolution of 1920x1080 or 2/3 of the maximum screen size. When the resolution of the display goes above 1080 pixels in height, the 2/3 maximum rule is applied. For example, if a 1920x1080 display is using the Broadcast Zone feature, it would be possible to resize the Zone to fill the entire screen. However, if a display of 1920x1081 were using the Broadcast Zone feature, the 2/3 maximum height rule would be applied and the Zone would be limited to a maximum height of 724 pixels.

Users are able to select a sub-region of the Prysm Digital Workplace (a portion of their display to broadcast video to participants as shared content) for output as VC content to an external codec or Skype for Business meeting.
